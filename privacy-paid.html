Privacy Policy - FoxBound Paid

The FoxBound Paid app does not contain ads and uses only Firebase Analytics to improve the service and Crashlytics for anonymous crash reporting.

Data Collected

  • Firebase Analytics: collects anonymous usage data, such as screen views, session duration, and user interactions to help improve the app.
  • Crashlytics: collects anonymous crash reports (stack trace, device model, Android version).

Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Analytics & Crashlytics)
  • Google Play Services

Data Security

We apply technical and organizational measures to protect data, but no Internet transmission is 100% secure.

Children

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 years old.
